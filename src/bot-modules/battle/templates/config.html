Max number of ladder battles: 
When laddering, the bot will try to search for battles until it is playing this number of ladder battles.
Then, it will wait for one battle to finish before searching for more.

The bot will forfeit if this limit of turns is reached. This will prevent players from stalling the bot with very long battles.
